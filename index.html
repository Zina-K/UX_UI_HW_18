My UX Skills

User Research

UX focuses on ease of use, efficiency, and making the process of using the product enjoyable for the users. Through research and testing, problems that users face can be identified, solved, and verified.

Responsive Design

An approach to web design that aims to create a flexible and adaptable design that can adjust to different screen sizes and resolutions, without sacrificing usability or functionality. This provides optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices.

Interaction Design

Design that focuses on creating meaningful and engaging experiences for users when they interact with the product or service, designing the way users interact with technology, considering factors like usability, accessibility, and aesthetics.
